Welcome, dear friend! In this game you will arrange items in a happy home, sort what makes a true education, and pick the right pieces of healthy living.

Take your time. Have fun. Every answer teaches something.

How To Play

1. Choose a game: Home, Education, or Health.

2. You will see a picture with different areas, and a tray of items on the right.

3. Tap an item to pick it up, then tap the area where it belongs.

4. If you wish, you can also drag items with your mouse.

5. A correct placement gives you +10 points. A wrong one costs 5 seconds off the clock.

6. Place all items before time runs out for a big bonus!
